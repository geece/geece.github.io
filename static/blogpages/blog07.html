施工相關2020.12.17

2020 Supplier Sustainability Award Gold-GREEN VALUE
2020 全球永續發展計畫金獎 - 綠豐

永續發展是綠豐公司的持續目標 ，我們提供穩定的冷凍冷藏及機電運轉設備，為客戶節省能源是我們的核心價值。2020年我們協助客戶申請能源局補助375萬元更新磁浮離心冰水主機，節能率達37.1%，預計節省電費金額約410萬元，冷凍冷藏及空調的能耗減少822噸溫室氣體排放，相當於68,500棵樹木一年的碳吸收量。

2020 年對於每個人都是充滿挑戰的一年，面臨著供應鏈的巨大變化和困難，但是我們也從這個巨變中學到很多，並即時進行調整以度過今年的危機成為轉機，不僅供應商，連同客戶也在學習如何在彈性供應鏈中取得平衡。我認為公司永續發展節約能源越來越重要，我們與所有合作夥伴一起朝這個目標邁進。感謝與台灣裕利藥品公司參加全球永續發展計畫得到金牌的獎項。

Sustainable development is a continuous goal for Green Value, we provide stable running Chiller、Refrigeration equipment & electromechanical device, Saving energy for our clients is the core value for us.

In 2020, we assisted customers to apply for a subsidy of 3.75 million NT$ from the Energy Administration to update Active Magnetic Centrifugal Chiller which energy saving rate reached 37.1%. It is estimated that the electricity bill will be saved by about 4.1 million NT$. The energy consumption from chiller、refrigeration and air conditioning will reduce 822 tons of greenhouse gas emissions, which is equivalent to The annual carbon absorption of 68,500 trees.

2020 is a challenging year for everyone, we encounter the huge change and difficulty of supply chains, but we also learn a lot from this great change and make adjustment in time to pass the crisis of this year.
Not only the supplier but also the clients are learning how to get balance in resilience supply chains.
In my opinion, sustainability is being more important that we will make effort and heading to this goal with our all partners.

Appreciate TW Zuellig Pharma provide opportunity for us to participate Global Sustainable Development Project and won Gold Award together.
